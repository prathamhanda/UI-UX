About Saturnalia

Saturnalia is the annual techno-cultural festival of Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala. It's a vibrant three-day extravaganza of technology, culture, and creativity, attracting students from across the nation. From intense coding competitions and robotic battles to mesmerizing dance-offs and soul-stirring musical nights, Saturnalia is a celebration of talent and youthful energy. Join us for an unforgettable experience!
