Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in Saturnalia? +

Saturnalia is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from any college or university across India.

Is there a registration fee? +

Yes, there is a nominal registration fee for participation in most events. The fee structure is detailed on the registration page for each specific event.

Will accommodation be provided for outstation participants? +

Yes, we provide accommodation facilities within the campus hostels for outstation participants on a first-come, first-served basis. Please indicate your requirement during registration.
